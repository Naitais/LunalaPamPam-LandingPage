Hello World!

My name is Pam, I am an Argentinian artist. I manage traditional painting techniques such as watercolor or more modern techniques such as digital drawing. Whenever I face a blank canvas, I take inspiration from cats and fantasy-themed stuff (mainly from cats, lol). I love creating art! The best feeling for me is showing the world my creativity with each work of art I finish. I hope you enjoy taking a look at my website! Do not forget to check out the gallery section, there you will find most of my works.
